Architecture

Axismundi separates evidence, rules, ontology, bindings, products, and automation into six repository layers.

corpus/
source documents and refined references
atlas/
rule-based knowledge and audits
core/
formal platform and design-system ontologies
bindings/
cross-ontology translation
products/
reference implementations and future distributables
tools/
validators, generators, and builders
