# Omphalos Asset Licenses

This file records per-file licensing for non-code assets bundled with Omphalos.
Theme code and templates are licensed under GPL-3.0-or-later unless a more
specific notice applies.

## Fonts

Bundled font files under `fonts/` are distributed under the SIL Open Font
License 1.1. Each font family directory includes a verbatim `OFL.txt` and a
`source.txt` with source and processing notes.

## Icons

Material Symbols Outlined under `icons/material-symbols-outlined/` is licensed
under the Apache License 2.0. The license text is preserved at
`icons/material-symbols-outlined/LICENSE.txt`.

## Development Media

Development-only media placeholders under `assets/media/` are excluded from the
WordPress.org distribution ZIP by `.distignore`. They are documented in
`assets/media/README.md` for local VQA and seed usage.
